Complete logistics Solution

An enormous amount of knowledge and expertise empowers Ratha Shipping Agencies to extend comprehensive Project Cargo Logistics services, endorsed by customers who have entrusted us with their most complex shipments. This globally offered service is executed with meticulous planning and detailed preparation. From dismantling at the origin to the ultimate installation at the receiver’s site, every aspect of the project is managed with care and precision by Ratha Shipping Agencies.

"Passionate about projects" is our motto, ensuring the safe, sound, and timely delivery of any project consignment, no matter how big, bulky, or non-standard the cargo may be. Ratha Shipping Agencies possesses the efficiency and expertise to ‘make things happen’ in the realm of logistics.
